## India Used Car Price Prediction Dataset Description

Used Car Price Prediction is a regression problem aimed at predicting the selling price of a used car based on its features. The dataset contains various attributes describing each car’s characteristics, which are used to estimate its market value.

# The dataset contains the following information for each car:
Variable                       |   Description 
-------------------------------|----------------------------------
Brand                          | The manufacturer of the car (e.g., Maruti, Hyundai, Honda).
Car Model Name                 | The specific model of the car (e.g., Alto 800 LXi, i20, City).
Year of Manufacture            | The year the car was manufactured.
Kilometers Driven (kms_driven) | The total distance the car has traveled, in kilometers.
Fuel Type                      | The type of fuel the car uses (e.g., Petrol, Diesel, CNG, Electric).
City                           | The city or location where the car is listed for sale.
Price (Target Variable)        | The selling price of the car, which the model aims to predict.

These features capture important aspects that influence a car’s resale value, such as age, mileage, fuel type, and location. By training a machine learning model on this dataset, it is possible to predict used car prices accurately, helping buyers and sellers make informed decisions.
